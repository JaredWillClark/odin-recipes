Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a culinary masterpiece, layering rich, hearty meat sauce and creamy cheese between tender sheets of pasta, all topped with a golden, bubbling layer of mozzarella and Parmesan. Each bite offers a symphony of flavors, perfectly balancing the robustness of garlic and herbs with the smooth, indulgent cheeses, making it a dish that's both comforting and irresistibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodle
  • ground meat
  • Onion
  • garlic
  • tomato paste
  • cheese
  • egg
  • spices
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make spice mixture
  4. layer lasagna accordingly
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let lasagna rest before serving
